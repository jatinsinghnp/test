Ascend Accounting & Advisory Co.Ltd is a professional accounting and auditing firm licensed by Accounting and Auditing Regulator (“AAAC”), a professional tax agent licensed by General Department of Taxation (“GDT”), and a member of Kampuchea Institute of Certified Public Accountants and Auditors (“KICPAA”).   1. Aduit associate

  5. Aduit Intern

1) Audit associate:

Job description:

  • Performs audit work at the Client’s office for the assigned audit cycles; follows audit techniques suggested and considers work paper documentation required by assistant managers/senior associates in documenting the audit work done.
  • Reports to assistant managers/senior associates any audit/accounting issues noted for a client and discuss/proposes alternative solutions to the issue noted.
  • Ensures that all overtime work is pre-approved by assistant managers/senior associates by completing an overtime form sheet.
  • Resolves all audit partner/manager/assistant manager/senior associate review notes.

Job requirements:

  • Education: University degree, preferably in Auditing/Accounting or business-related fields
  • Experience: Fresh graduate or a minimum of one year of recent external audit service and dealing in teamwork.
  • Good working knowledge of Ms. word, excel, PowerPoint, and searching skills.

To Apply:
